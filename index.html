About the Lab

Psychology Meets Performance

Mindful Athlete Lab was founded on a simple belief: every athlete deserves access to the mental training tools that elite performers use.

We combine evidence-based sport psychology with mindfulness practices to help teenage and college athletes navigate the psychological demands of competition, injury recovery, performance anxiety, and the pressure to succeed.

Our approach is grounded in research but delivered with empathy, meeting each athlete exactly where they are in their journey.

Our Method

The Mindful Athlete Framework

A systematic approach to building mental resilience and peak performance psychology.

01

Assess & Understand

We identify your unique mental game challenges, strengths, and goals through collaborative discussion and assessment.

02

Build Skills

Learn practical psychological skills: mindful breathing, visualization, self-talk management, focus training, and emotional regulation.

03

Practice & Apply

Integrate these skills into your training and competition through structured practice and real-world application.

04

Refine & Grow

Continuously adapt and evolve your mental training as you progress, ensuring sustained growth and resilience.
